# Food Safety Compliance

---

## Regulatory Framework

| Regulation | Region | Key Requirements |
|:-----------|:------|:----------------|
| FSMA (FSVP) | USA | Foreign Supplier Verification Program |
| EU Food Law 178/2002 | EU | Traceability, RASFF notification |
| China Food Safety Law | China | Registration, labeling, testing |
| Codex Alimentarius | International | General principles, commodity standards |

## HACCP Plan Summary

### Critical Control Points (CCPs)

| CCP | Hazard | Control | Critical Limit | Monitoring |
|:----|:-------|:--------|:--------------|:-----------|
| Metal Detection | Physical | Inline metal detector | No ferrous ≥ 1.0mm, non-ferrous ≥ 1.5mm | Every 30 min test |
| Nitrogen Flush | Chemical (oxidation) | N₂ generator + O₂ analyzer | Residual O₂ < 2% | Every batch |
| Drying | Biological | Fluid bed dryer temp | Product temp ≥ 70°C for 3 min | Continuous |
| Optical Sorting | Physical | Camera/sorter | Defect removal ≥ 95% | Hourly verification |

## Allergen Management

- Dedicated production lines for flaxseed-only products
- Comprehensive cleaning between runs
- Allergen testing (ELISA) on shared equipment
- Clear label declaration per regulatory requirements
- Supplier allergen declarations on all raw materials

## Recall Plan

1. **Identification** — Customer complaint or QC finding triggers investigation
2. **Containment** — Affected lot placed on hold within 1 hour
3. **Traceability** — Full lot genealogy from raw material to customer
4. **Notification** — Customers notified within 4 hours of confirmed issue
5. **Corrective Action** — Root cause and CAPA within 48 hours
6. **Mock Recall** — Conducted annually, documented
