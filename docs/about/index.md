# About FlaxseedsPro

---

## Organisation

FlaxseedsPro is a technically-driven supplier of flaxseed fractions to the food, nutritional, and industrial ingredient markets. Our facility integrates cleaning, dehulling, milling, oil pressing, gum extraction, and packaging under a single HACCP and ISO 22000 certified roof.

## Core Capabilities

| Capability | Detail |
|:-----------|:--------|
| Products | Hull, kernel, powder, gum, oil — each with complete technical characterisation |
| Capacity | 8 MT/hr intake, 3,000 MT annual finished product output |
| Certifications | HACCP / ISO 22000 / FSSC 22000 / Organic / Non-GMO / Kosher / Gluten-Free |
| Quality | On-site lab with NIR, GC-FID, HPLC, Soxhlet, Kjeldahl, microbiology |
| R&D | Applications lab for customer formulation support |
