# Omega-3 in Plant-Based Diets

**FlaxseedsPro White Paper | 2026**

---

## Executive Summary

As global adoption of plant-based diets accelerates, ensuring adequate omega-3 intake from non-animal sources has become a critical nutritional consideration. Flaxseed, with its 52-57% ALA content, represents the most concentrated and cost-effective plant-based omega-3 source available.

## Key Findings

- ALA from flaxseed converts to EPA at 5-10% efficiency and DHA at 1-5%
- 30g (2 tbsp) of milled flaxseed provides 6-7g of ALA — exceeding daily recommendations
- Ground flaxseed has 3x better ALA bioavailability than whole seed
- Refrigeration of ground flaxseed preserves ALA content for 6-9 months

## Recommendations for Plant-Based Diets

1. Daily intake: 1-2 tbsp milled flaxseed (15-30g) or 1 tbsp flaxseed oil (15ml)
2. Store milled flaxseed in refrigerator after opening
3. Add to smoothies, oatmeal, yogurt alternatives, and baked goods
4. Consider flaxseed oil supplementation for higher ALA needs
