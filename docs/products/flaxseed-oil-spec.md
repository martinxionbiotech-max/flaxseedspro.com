## 10. Storage and Shelf Life

| Condition | FSP-OIL-001 | FSP-OIL-002 |
|---|---|---|
| Temperature | < 20 °C (cool, dark) | < 25 °C (ambient, dark) |
| Headspace protection | Nitrogen blanket mandatory | Nitrogen blanket recommended |
| Light protection | Opaque or amber packaging | Opaque or amber packaging |
| Relative humidity | < 65 % | < 65 % |
| Storage orientation | Upright | Upright |
| Shelf life (sealed, ≤ 20 °C) | 18 months from production | 24 months from production |
| Shelf life (after opening) | 3 months refrigerated | 6 months refrigerated |

*Storage conditions apply to unopened, manufacturer-sealed containers. Once opened, oil should be refrigerated (2–8 °C) and used promptly. Do not freeze. Avoid oxygen exposure — re-purge headspace with N₂ after each use where possible.*
