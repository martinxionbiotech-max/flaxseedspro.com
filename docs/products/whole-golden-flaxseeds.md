# Whole Golden Flaxseeds

**Product Code:** FSP-GLD-001

---

## Description

Our premium whole golden flaxseeds offer the same exceptional nutritional profile as brown flaxseeds with a milder, nuttier flavor and lighter appearance. Ideal for applications where visual appearance is critical — bakery toppings, cereal blends, and retail packaging.

## Packaging

- 25 kg multi-ply paper bags
- 1 MT super sacks (bulk bags)
- 20 MT bulk container liners

## Storage

- Store in cool, dry conditions (< 25°C, < 60% RH)
- Away from direct sunlight and strong odors
- Shelf life: 12 months from production date

> **Note:** Golden flaxseeds are more sensitive to light exposure than brown. Store in opaque packaging when possible.
