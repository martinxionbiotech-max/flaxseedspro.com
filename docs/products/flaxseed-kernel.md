# Flaxseed Kernel

**Product Code:** FSP-KRN-001  
**Revision:** V11.0

---

## Description

Flaxseed kernel is the lipid- and protein-rich fraction obtained after mechanical dehulling. Compared to whole seed, the kernel fraction concentrates oil and protein while reducing fiber. It is the preferred feed stock for cold-pressed oil production and protein isolate manufacturing.
