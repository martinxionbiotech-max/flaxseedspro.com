# Flaxseed Hull

**Product Code:** FSP-HUL-001  
**Revision:** V11.0

---

## Description

Flaxseed hull is the outer seed coat fraction obtained through mechanical dehulling and air classification. It is the richest known botanical source of **secoisolariciresinol diglucoside (SDG)** lignans and soluble dietary fiber. The hull fraction is produced without chemical treatment, preserving native functionality.
