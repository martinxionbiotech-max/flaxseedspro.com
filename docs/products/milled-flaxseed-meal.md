# Milled Flaxseed Meal

**Product Code:** FSP-MLM-001

---

## Description

Our milled flaxseed meal is produced from freshly cleaned whole flaxseeds, ground using low-temperature hammer milling to preserve nutritional integrity. The milling process breaks the seed coat, making nutrients more bioavailable. Available in fine (0.5-1.0 mm) and medium (1.0-2.0 mm) grind sizes.

## Storage & Shelf Life

- Store in cool, dry conditions (< 25°C, < 60% RH)
- Nitrogen-flushed packaging extends freshness
- Shelf life: 6 months ambient, 9 months refrigerated
- Once opened, use within 30 days

> **Stabilization:** Our meal is stabilized using nitrogen flushing immediately after milling to prevent oxidation and preserve the omega-3 profile.
