# Flaxseed Oil

**Product Code:** FSP-OIL-001 (Cold-Pressed), FSP-OIL-002 (Refined)  
**Revision:** V11.0

---

## Description

Flaxseed oil is produced by mechanical cold pressing of flaxseed kernels at temperatures below 40°C, preserving the native fatty acid profile and antioxidant content. Available as **cold-pressed (unrefined)** and **physically refined** grades.
