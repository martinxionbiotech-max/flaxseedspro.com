# Cold-Pressed Flaxseed Oil

**Product Code:** FSP-OIL-001

---

## Description

Our cold-pressed flaxseed oil is extracted using mechanical pressing at temperatures below 40°C, preserving the natural omega-3 profile and antioxidant content. The oil is processed without solvents and is nitrogen-flushed to prevent oxidation. Available in raw and refined grades.

## Storage & Shelf Life

- Store in cool, dark conditions (< 20°C recommended)
- Oxygen-sensitive — keep sealed until use
- Refrigeration extends shelf life significantly
- Shelf life: 18 months raw, 24 months refined (unopened)
- Refrigerate after opening, use within 60 days
