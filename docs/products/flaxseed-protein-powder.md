# Flaxseed Protein Powder

**Product Code:** FSP-PRO-001

---

## Description

Our flaxseed protein powder is produced by mechanical fractionation of defatted flaxseed meal. With 40-50% protein content, it offers a complete amino acid profile rich in arginine and glutamic acid. The powder is light in color with a mild flavor, making it ideal for plant-based protein blends, baked goods, and nutritional supplements.
