# Whole Brown Flaxseeds

**Product Code:** FSP-BRN-001

---

## Description

Our premium whole brown flaxseeds are sourced from select growing regions known for optimal omega-3 profiles. Rich in alpha-linolenic acid (ALA), dietary fiber, and lignans, brown flaxseeds are the industry standard for nutritional oilseed applications.
