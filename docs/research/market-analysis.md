## Key Trends

1. **Plant-Based Protein Demand** — Growing demand for flaxseed protein in meat alternatives and plant-based products
2. **Omega-3 Fortification** — Food manufacturers adding flaxseed to breads, cereals, and snacks
3. **Organic Growth** — Organic flaxseed market growing at 12% CAGR
4. **Clean Label** — Flaxseed as a natural egg replacer (mucilage) gaining traction
5. **Regional Shift** — Increasing production in Kazakhstan and Russia

## Price Factors

- Global supply from Canada and Kazakhstan
- Quality premiums (organic +15-25%, high ALA +5-10%)
- Freight costs and container availability
- Currency fluctuations (CAD, USD, EUR)
- Crop quality reports during harvest season (Aug-Oct)
