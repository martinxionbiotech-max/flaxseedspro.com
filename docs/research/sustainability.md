## Environmental Commitment

At FlaxseedsPro, we are committed to sustainable production practices that minimize environmental impact while delivering premium quality products.

**Target:** 30% reduction by 2030 (baseline 2023)

## Sustainability Initiatives

### Water Conservation
- Closed-loop cooling system in oil extraction
- Water recycling rate: 85%
- Annual water usage: 12,000 m³ (industry avg: 18,000 m³)

### Waste Reduction
- Seed hulls diverted to animal feed: 98%
- Oil press cake used as protein ingredient: 100%
- Packaging waste recycled: 92%
- Landfill diversion rate: 95%

### Energy Efficiency
- Solar panel installation: 200 kW capacity
- Energy-efficient motors (IE3/IE4) across facility
- Heat recovery from drying operations
- LED lighting throughout (saved 40% lighting energy)

## Sustainable Sourcing

- Direct relationships with growers practicing crop rotation
- Flaxseed requires 30% less water than almonds per kg
- Flax as a rotational crop improves soil health
- Non-GMO varieties preserve biodiversity
- Organic program supports regenerative agriculture
