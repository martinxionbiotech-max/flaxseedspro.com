# Drying & Packaging Equipment

---

## Overview

Our drying and packaging line ensures product stability and shelf life through precise moisture control and oxygen-free packaging.

## Equipment List

| Equipment | Model | Capacity | Purpose |
|:----------|:-----:|:--------:|:--------|
| Fluid Bed Dryer | FD-1000 | 3 MT/hr | Moisture reduction |
| Rotary Dryer | RD-800 | 2 MT/hr | Bulk drying |
| Nitrogen Flush Tunnel | NFT-400 | — | Modified atmosphere packaging |
| Vertical Bag Filler | VBF-600 | 600 bph | Bag filling and sealing |
| Bulk Load-out Station | BLS-200 | 40 MT/hr | Bulk container loading |
| Palletizer | PL-100 | 30 pallets/hr | Automated palletizing |

## Process Flow

```
Product → Fluid Bed Dryer → Cooler → Metal Detector → Nitrogen Flush → Bag Filler → Palletizer
```

## Key Specs

- Drying capacity: 3 MT/hr
- Moisture reduction: Up to 3%
- Final moisture control: ±0.2%
- Packaging formats: 10kg - 25kg bags, bulk totes, drums
- Nitrogen residual oxygen: < 2%
