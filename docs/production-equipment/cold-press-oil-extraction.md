# Cold Press Oil Extraction Equipment

---

## Overview

Our cold press line uses mechanical screw pressing at temperatures below 40°C to extract high-quality flaxseed oil without solvents. The system includes filtration, polishing, and nitrogen-flushed packaging.

## Equipment List

| Equipment | Model | Capacity | Purpose |
|:----------|:-----:|:--------:|:--------|
| Screw Press | SP-300 | 500 kg/hr | Oil extraction |
| Plate Filter | PF-200 | 500 L/hr | Primary filtration |
| Polish Filter | PF-100 | 500 L/hr | Final polishing |
| Nitrogen Generator | NG-50 | — | Oxygen-free packaging |
| Bottling Line | BL-200 | 200 bph | Automated filling/capping |
| Drum Filler | DF-100 | 20 drums/hr | Bulk container filling |

## Process Flow

```
Clean Seed → Screw Press → Crude Oil → Plate Filter → Polish Filter → Packaging
```

## Key Specs

- Extraction temperature: < 40°C
- Oil yield: 32-38% (vs. 35-42% solvent extraction)
- Residual oil in meal: 8-12%
- Filtration clarity: < 5 NTU
- Peroxide value: ≤ 2.0 meq/kg
