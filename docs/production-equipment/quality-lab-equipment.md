# Quality Lab Equipment

---

## Overview

Our on-site quality control laboratory is equipped with advanced analytical instrumentation for raw material, in-process, and finished product testing.

## Equipment List

| Equipment | Model | Tests Performed |
|:----------|:-----:|:----------------|
| NIR Analyzer | NIR-5000 | Moisture, protein, fat, fiber (rapid screening) |
| Gas Chromatograph | GC-2010 | Fatty acid profile (ALA content) |
| HPLC System | HPLC-300 | Lignan (SDG) quantification |
| Soxhlet Extractor | SE-400 | Total fat content |
| Kjeldahl Analyzer | KA-200 | Protein content |
| Muffle Furnace | MF-100 | Ash content |
| Moisture Balance | MB-50 | Rapid moisture |
| Water Activity Meter | AW-30 | Water activity (a_w) |
| Texture Analyzer | TA-XT2 | Seed hardness, meal texture |
| Spectrophotometer | UV-1800 | Color measurement, antioxidant assays |
