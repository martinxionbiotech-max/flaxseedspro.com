# Storage & Conveying Equipment

---

## Overview

Our material handling system ensures gentle, dust-controlled transport of flaxseeds throughout the facility, with dedicated storage for raw materials, intermediate products, and finished goods.

## Equipment List

| Equipment | Model | Capacity | Purpose |
|:----------|:-----:|:--------:|:--------|
| Grain Silos | SILO-500 (×4) | 500 MT each | Raw seed storage |
| Intermediate Bins | BIN-50 (×6) | 50 MT each | Process buffer storage |
| Finished Product Silos | SILO-200 (×3) | 200 MT each | Final product holding |
| Bucket Elevators | BE-100 (×8) | 10 MT/hr | Vertical conveying |
| Screw Conveyors | SC-200 (×12) | 5 MT/hr | Horizontal conveying |
| Pneumatic Conveyor | PC-150 | 3 MT/hr | Gentle product transport |
| Dust Collection System | DCS-5000 | 5000 CFM | Air filtration |

## Key Specs

- Total raw seed storage: 2,000 MT
- Total intermediate storage: 300 MT
- Total finished product storage: 600 MT
- Dust collection efficiency: > 99.9%
- Conveying speed: Variable, max 10 MT/hr
