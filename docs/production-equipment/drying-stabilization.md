# Drying & Stabilization

**CCP-4: Moisture Control & Anti-Oxidation**

---

## Process Objective

Reduce product moisture to target levels while minimising oxidative damage. Multiple drying technologies are available for different product streams.

## Drying Technologies

| Technology | Principle | Temperature Range | Capacity | Energy Consumption | Application |
|:-----------|:----------|:----------------:|:--------:|:-----------------:|:------------|
| Fluidized bed | Hot air suspension | 60–90°C | 3 MT/hr | 1.2–1.8 kWh/kg H₂O | Kernel, hull |
| Microwave | Dielectric heating | 40–60°C | 1 MT/hr | 0.8–1.2 kWh/kg H₂O | Powder (low temp) |
| Steam tube | Indirect rotary | 70–100°C | 2 MT/hr | 1.0–1.5 kWh/kg H₂O | Bulk drying |
| Infrared | Radiant heating | 50–80°C | 0.5 MT/hr | 1.5–2.0 kWh/kg H₂O | Thin-layer |

## Technology Comparison

| Factor | Fluidized Bed | Microwave | Steam Tube | IR |
|:-------|:------------:|:---------:|:----------:|:--:|
| Capital cost | Medium | High | Medium | Low |
| Operating cost | Low | High | Low | Medium |
| Product temp rise | 20–30°C | 5–10°C | 25–40°C | 15–25°C |
| Oxidative impact | Medium | Low | Medium-High | Medium |
| Moisture uniformity | Good | Excellent | Good | Fair |
| Throughput | High | Medium | High | Low |

## Recommended Settings by Product

| Product | Technology | Target Moisture | Max Product Temp | 
|:--------|:-----------|:--------------:|:----------------:|
| Whole seed / kernel | Fluidized bed | ≤ 8.0% | 45°C |
| Hull | Fluidized bed | ≤ 6.0% | 50°C |
| Full-fat powder | Microwave | ≤ 5.0% | 35°C |
| Defatted powder | Fluidized bed | ≤ 6.0% | 60°C |
| Gum | Spray dryer | ≤ 5.0% | 80°C (outlet air) |

## CCP-4 Monitoring

| Product | Check | Frequency | Critical Limit | Action |
|:--------|:------|:---------:|:--------------|:-------|
| All | Moisture | Every 30 min | Per spec ± 0.5% | Adjust residence time |
| Oil-containing | Peroxide value | Every batch | ≤ 2.0 meq/kg (full-fat) | Reduce temperature |
| All | Product temperature | Continuous | Per spec max | Reduce heating |
