# Drying & Stabilisation Line — Equipment Specification

## 1. Process Objective

Reduce moisture content of flaxseed (from harvest-wet 14–18 % down to ≤ 8 % storage moisture) while maintaining seed viability (germination ≥ 85 % for whole seed) and preventing oxidative rancidity. The line also stabilises partially-processed fractions (press cake, gum cake) prior to storage or further processing. Four independent drying technologies provide operational flexibility and allow matching energy efficiency to product state. This station is designated **CCP-4** (Critical Control Point — Moisture & Temperature Control).

---

## 2. Equipment Configuration & Flow Diagram

```
Wet Flaxseed / Intermediate Product
    │
    ├───→ [ Fluidised Bed Dryer ] ───→ (Whole seed, 60–90 °C, 3 MT/hr)
    │                                      │
    │                                      ▼
    ├───→ [ Microwave Dryer ] ───→ (Germination-sensitive seed, 40–60 °C, 1 MT/hr)
    │                                      │
    │                                      ▼
    ├───→ [ Steam Tube Rotary Dryer ] ───→ (Press cake / bulk, 70–100 °C, 2 MT/hr)
    │                                      │
    │                                      ▼
    └───→ [ Infrared Dryer ] ───→ (Gum cake / thin-layer, 50–80 °C, 0.5 MT/hr)
                                           │
                                           ▼
                                [ Tempering / Cooling Bins ]
                                           │
                                           ▼
                                Dry Product (≤ 8 % moisture)
                                           │
                                           ▼
                                [ Storage Silos / Bag-off ]
```

## 6. HACCP — CCP-4 Integration

| CCP | Hazard                         | Critical Limit                       | Monitoring                | Corrective Action                          |
|-----|--------------------------------|--------------------------------------|---------------------------|--------------------------------------------|
| 4a  | **Product temp. exceeded** → oxidative rancidity, nutrient loss | Per dryer limits (see §5)             | Continuous temp. logging  | Reduce heat input / increase feed rate; inspect product quality |
| 4b  | **Moisture > 8 %** → mould growth during storage | Outlet MC ≤ 8 % (w/w)                | Online NIR (every 2 min)  | Re-circulate batch; increase residence time or temp. |
| 4c  | **O₂ ingress in rotary dryer** → fire / auto-oxidation | Sweep gas O₂ ≤ 3 %                    | O₂ analyser (continuous)  | Increase N₂ purge; check seals; emergency stop |
| 4d  | **Dust explosion** (FBD, rotary) | Housekeeping; ATEX-rated equipment    | Visual + pressure sensors | Automatic deluge; shut down air; evacuate  |

## 10. Safety & Compliance

- **ATEX Zone 22**: FBD outlet, rotary dryer discharge, dust collection hoppers — combustible dust rated equipment.
- **Microwave leakage**: Annual survey per IEC 62311 — limit 5 mW/cm² at 5 cm.
- **Rotary dryer N₂ purge**: Interlocked — if O₂ > 3 % or N₂ flow < 50 Nm³/hr, heat shuts down automatically.
- **Hot surface guarding**: All dryers have insulated cladding + hazard warning labels (> 60 °C surfaces guarded).
- **LOTO**: Lockout for rotary dryer tube bundle access, microwave magnetron maintenance, FBD bed access.
- **PPE**: Heat-resistant gloves for product sampling; hearing protection in FBD/rotary area (> 85 dB(A)).
