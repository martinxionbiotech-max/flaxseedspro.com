# Production Equipment

Our facility is equipped with state-of-the-art processing machinery designed for optimal flaxseed handling from intake to finished product. All equipment meets food-grade sanitary standards and is part of our HACCP-controlled processing environment.

---

## Equipment Categories

<div class="grid cards" markdown>

-   :material-sort: **Cleaning & Sorting** — Gravity separation, optical sorting, magnetic separation
-   :material-shell: **Dehulling System** — Mechanical dehulling and aspiration
-   :material-grinder: **Milling & Grinding** — Low-temperature hammer mills
-   :material-oil: **Cold Press Oil Extraction** — Screw press, filtration, bottling
-   :material-dryer: **Drying & Packaging** — Fluid bed dryers, nitrogen flush, bagging
-   :material-test-tube: **Quality Lab Equipment** — NIR analyzers, GC, moisture balances
-   :material-warehouse: **Storage & Conveying** — Silos, conveyors, dust collection
-   :material-cog: **Automation & Control** — PLC systems, SCADA, batch tracking

</div>
