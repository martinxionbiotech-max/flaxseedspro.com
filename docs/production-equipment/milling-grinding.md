# Milling & Grinding Equipment

---

## Overview

Our milling system uses low-temperature hammer milling technology to produce flaxseed meal while keeping product temperatures below 45°C, preserving omega-3 integrity.

## Equipment List

| Equipment | Model | Capacity | Purpose |
|:----------|:-----:|:--------:|:--------|
| Hammer Mill | HM-600 | 2 MT/hr | Primary grinding |
| Fine Grind Mill | FG-400 | 1 MT/hr | Fine particle reduction |
| Sifter | SF-800 | 2 MT/hr | Particle size classification |
| Cooling Conveyor | CC-300 | 2 MT/hr | Post-mill temperature reduction |

## Grind Options

| Grind Size | Mesh Range | Application |
|:-----------|:----------:|:------------|
| Coarse | 8-16 mesh | Bakery blends |
| Medium | 16-30 mesh | Cereal additions |
| Fine | 30-60 mesh | Smoothies, protein powders |
| Ultra-fine | 60-100 mesh | Nutritional supplements |

## Key Specs

- Maximum throughput: 2 MT/hr
- Temperature rise: < 15°C above ambient
- Particle size accuracy: ±5% of target
- Cleaning interval: Every 200 MT or 8 hrs
