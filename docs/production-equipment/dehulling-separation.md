# Dehulling & Separation

---

## Process Objective

Remove the fibrous outer hull from flaxseed kernels to produce two value-added fractions: **hull** (fiber + lignan concentrate) and **kernel** (oil + protein concentrate).

## Equipment Configuration

```
Clean Seed → Conditioning → Dehuller → Hull/Kernel Mixture → Air Classifier → Gravity Separator → Hull Stream / Kernel Stream
```

## Dehulling Technologies

| Method | Principle | Efficiency | Capacity | Oil Loss |
|:-------|:----------|:----------:|:--------:|:--------:|
| Centrifugal impact | Rotor 3000–4500 rpm | 85–90% | 3 MT/hr | < 2% |
| Roller gap | Counter-rotating rolls | 80–85% | 4 MT/hr | < 1% |

## Separation Equipment

| Unit | Type | Model | Capacity | Separation Basis |
|:-----|:-----|:------|:--------:|:----------------|
| Air classifier | Zigzag | ZAC-800 | 2 MT/hr | Terminal velocity (hull 1–2 m/s, kernel 4–6 m/s) |
| Gravity separator | Deck type | GDS-1000 | 1.5 MT/hr | Density (hull 0.4, kernel 0.6 g/mL) |

## Performance Targets

| Fraction | Purity | Yield | Application |
|:---------|:------:|:----:|:------------|
| Hull stream | ≥ 92% hull | 30–35% of whole seed | Fiber enrichment, lignan extraction |
| Kernel stream | ≥ 95% kernel | 60–65% of whole seed | Oil pressing, protein production |

## Process Parameters

| Parameter | Set Point | Tolerance |
|:----------|:---------:|:---------:|
| Seed moisture (conditioning) | 8–9% | ± 0.5% |
| Dehuller rotor speed | 3500 rpm | ± 100 rpm |
| Air classifier velocity | 4.0 m/s | ± 0.3 m/s |
| Gravity deck angle | 6.5° | ± 0.5° |
